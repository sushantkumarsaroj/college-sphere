Uttaranchal University Highlights 2025

Uttaranchal University was established in 2005 and is located in Dehradun. Uttaranchal Technical University is recognised by the UGC, BCI, and PCI. The institute is approved by the AICTE and accredited with NAAC A+ Grade. Uttaranchal Technical University Dehradun offers UG, PG, and other level programmes across various streams such as Engineering, Law, Management, Nursing, Paramedical, Science and others. The flagship courses offered at Uttaranchal University Dehradun are BTech, BSc, and MBA.

Uttaranchal University Uttarakhand is ranked among the top 30 private colleges by the India Today 2023 ranking. The institute offers various types of scholarships to the students. The highest package offered at Uttaranchal University placement 2023 is INR 19.50 LPA. Uttaranchal Technical University appoints experienced and trained faculty members with years of experience. The institute has well-equipped infrastructure which includes labs, a library, an auditorium, a conference hall, a sports complex, a hostel, and others.
