ICFAI University

  • ICFAI University Dehradun admissions 2025 are open for all the programmes. The university offers a wide range of programmes to students at UG, PG, and PhD levels, mainly in fields such as Engineering, Law, Management, Education, etc. ICFAI Dehradun considers candidates’ merit scores and performance in PI for UG courses. ICFAI University Highlights 2025

ICFAI University Dehradun is a private college and was established in 2003 under the ICFAI University Act 2003 (Act No.16 of 2003) passed by the Uttaranchal Legislative Assembly. It is one of the leading universities in Uttarakhand and is a constituent university of the ICFAI group. The university is recognised by the UGC and is also a member of the Association of Commonwealth Universities, London, and the Association of Indian Universities, New Delhi. ICFAI University Dehradun Uttarakhand has also been approved by BCI, PCI, and NCTE, and offers AICTE-approved courses to students. ICFAI University Dehradun courses are offered to students at UG, PG, and PhD levels in various streams such as Engineering, Management, Law, Teaching, Science, Computer Applications, Commerce, etc. Some of the popular courses offered at ICFAI Dehradun are BTech, BBA, BCA, BA LLB, BBA LLB, BCom, BSc, MBA, MTech, MCA, MA, and many more. Further, the ICFAI University Dehradun Uttarakhand courses have also been approved by the Distance Education Council (DEC).

The flagship programmes offered at ICFAI Dehradun are BCom and MBA. The college offers these programmes to students at UG and PG levels. Further, the college offers these programmes for a duration of 3 and 2 years, respectively with a comprehensive curriculum. ICFAI University Dehradun Uttarakhand courses strive to enhance the learning experience of students to prepare them for the industry.
