IMS UNISON UNIVERSITY Highlights 2025

IMS Unison is a constituent of the Unison Education Foundation and is approved by UGC, The State Government, the Bar Council of India, and AICTE. IMS University was established in 1996 and through 5 schools of study, offers UG, PG, and PhD courses to students. These courses are provided across the Management, Law, and various other streams. It is a private university and is also a member of AIU. IMS Unison Dehradun is ranked 8th among the Top 40 Private Universities by Times of India Ranking 2024. The university is also ranked 8th among the Top 20 North B- Schools by Times of India Ranking 2024. IMS Dehradun is popular for its flagship programmes which are LLM and MBA. The college has a panel of guest lecturers to inspire students in their respective careers and to give them tips and invaluable insights in every course. Most importantly, IMS Dehradun courses are provided to students at reasonable fees, to enable students to acquire knowledge and skills in their preferred area of interest. The college also offers an excellent infrastructure to students The flagship programmes offered at IMS Unison University are BA LLB and MBA. The college offers these programmes to students at UG and PG levels. Further, the college offers these programmes for a duration of 5 and 2 years, respectively with a comprehensive curriculum. IMS Dehradun courses strive to enhance the learning experience of students in order to prepare them for the industry.
