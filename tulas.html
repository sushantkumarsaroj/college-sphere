TULA'S INSTITUTE Highlights 2025

Tula's Institute Dehradun is ranked 125th by India Today 2023 for BBA, 130th by India Today 2023 under the Engineering (Private) category, and 70th by Outlook 2023 under the Engineering (Private) category. Tula's Institute Dehradun is accredited with A+ Grade by NAAC and was established in 2006. The college was established under the care of Rishabh Educational Trust. Tula's Institute Dehradun is spread across 22 acre of land and has over 150 faculty members. Tula's Institute Dehradun is approved by AICTE.
