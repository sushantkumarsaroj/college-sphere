DIT UNIVERSITY Highlights 2025

DIT University's popular courses are BTech, MBA, MTech, and BArch. Admission to BTech is based on the applicant's JEE rank, NATA/JEE Paper-II for BArch, GATE (CS &IT)/UGC-NET/DUET for MTech, etc. The admission procedure hence varies for each programme. Apart from this, it is also crucial that the aspirants fulfil the course-specific eligibility requirements

Founded in 1998, Dehradun Institute of Technology (DIT) University is a private university in Dehradun, Uttarakhand. It is deemed university-approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Additionally, it is part of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and is the first University in Uttarakhand to receive QS I-Gauge E-Lead. DIT University admission office facilitates various courses in the streams of Management, Engineering, Arts and Sciences. Admission to the courses is through counselling based on merit in the last qualifying exam/accepted entrance exam. In DIT University placements 2022-23, over 1350 offers were made. Some of its top recruiters are CRED, Adobe, Blinkit, Zomato, Oracle, Amazon, etc
