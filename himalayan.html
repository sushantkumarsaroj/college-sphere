HIMALAYAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY Highlights 2025

Himalayan Institute of Technology, also known as HIT Doon, is a private institute located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand. The institute offers 21 courses such as MBA/PGDM, B.Sc, M.Sc, BCA, B.Com, BBA, BHM, M.Com and UG Diploma. These courses are offered across 10 specialisations with 60 seat intake. The total specialisations offered at the institute include Chemistry, Forestry, Genetics, Information Technology, Mathematics, Physics, Zoology. The courses offered at the institute are approved by AICTE. The institute offers UG and PG courses in Full Time mode. These Degree and Diploma courses are offered across multiple streams such as Business & Management Studies, IT & Software, Science, Engineering, Accounting & Commerce and Hospitality & Travel.

I have chosen this college by knowing about its faculty members from the seniors, and I also like the place where the college is situated. In our college, annual sports, fresher party and one important Radio Mirchi event are organised every year. I don't know about scholarships because I don't get any scholarships. However, I think for students of Uttarakhand, our college provides some scholarships.
