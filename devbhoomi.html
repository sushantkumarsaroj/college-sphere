DEV BHOOMI UTTARAKHAND UNIVERSITY Highlights 2025

Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University (DBUU) is a private university located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand. Spread over 42 acres of land, the University was established in 2005. DBUU is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC), Council of Architecture (CoA), Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), Indian Paramedical Council (IPC) and Indian Nursing Council (INC). The University provides a range of courses in Engineering, Management, Computer Application, Law, Pharmacy, Ayurveda - BAMS, Nursing, Fashion Design, Science and Humanities. These courses are provided through 11 schools of DBUU. The courses are provided in full-time mode at the UG and PG levels. Some of the popular programmes offered at the college are BA, MBA, and BSc.

Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand University is famous for its popular programmes, namely BA and MBA. These course are approved by UGC, COA, PCI, INC, UAU and IPC. The students of MBA programme are placed in top companies such as Nucleus Software, Jaro Education, and Ananda Group. MBA is provided in several specialisations including Agribusiness, Digital Marketing, HR / Marketing / Finance, Business Analytics, Logistics, and Supply Chain Management.
