BFIT GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS Highlights 2025

BFIT, also known as Baba Farid Institute of Technology, is a prominent institute located in the tranquil environs of Dehradun. The institute was established in 2002 and affiliated with HNB Garhwal Central University. The institute is approved by UGC, AICTE, NAAC, and NDRI. BFIT Dehradun caters to the academic needs of over 20,000 students. The institute offers UG, PG and diploma courses to students. These courses are provided across the Science, Pharmacy, Nursing, Management, Engineering and various other streams. The flagship courses offered at BFIT University Dehradun are BTech and MBA. BFIT Dehradun appoints experienced and skilled faculty members. The institute facilitates cost-free career guidance to students through a collaboration with CBC, which is a Dubai-based company that ensures 100% job placement. The institute offers scholarships to the meritorious students. BFIT Dehradun has well-equipped infrastructure which includes classrooms, labs, a library, an auditorium, a cafeteria, a hostel, and others.
