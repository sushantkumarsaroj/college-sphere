MAYA DEVI UNIVERSITY Highlights 2025

Maya Devi University is a private university established in 2010 under the Uttarakhand Private University (Amendment) Act 2024, (Act No. – 05, 2024). He university is recognised by the UGC. Moreover, Maya Devi University is also approved by the AICTE and PCI. The university provides various undergraduate, postgraduate, and diploma programmes under 11 schools of study. The names of Maya Devi University schools include School of Sciences, School of Pharmacy, School of Agriculture, School of Engineering, School of Management & Commerce, School of Skills & Vocational Studies, School of Hotel Management & Tourism, School of Animation & Computer Application, School of Journalism & Mass Communication, School of Holistic Studies & Disaster Management, and School of Fashion Designing & Lifestyle Management. Some of the courses offered through these schools include BTech, MBA, BSc, MSc, BPharm, DPharm, BDes, BFA, BCA, MCA, BBA, BCom, MCom, and various diploma and PG diploma programmes. Furthermore, the top recruiters of the university include Flipkart, Wipro, TCS, Infosys, Accenture, HCL, and Taj among others
