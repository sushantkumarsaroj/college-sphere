  • The University has released the placement stats on its official website, regardless of year. Hence, considering it for 2024. As per the details, 192 companies visited the University campus in 2024 and placed 872 students. The highest package offered during Quantum University placements 2024 was INR 33.50 LPA and the average package stood at INR 7.20 LPA. The list of recruiters included Oracle, Adobe, Capgemini, and many others.
  • The NIRF report 2024 has also been released for Engineering and Management. As per the report, 148 BTech and 7 MTech students were placed during the 2023 drive. Furthermore, 21 MBA students were placed during Quantum University placements 2023 and the median package offered was INR 4.57 LPA.
