Shivalik College of Engineering Highlights 2025

A premier college in higher education, SCE - Shivalik College of Engineering has been grounded in Dehradun, Uttarakhand since 2008 under the Rijan Education Society. It is a non-profit organisation that aims to focus on world-class education and generate resources for providing and supporting quality education for students. SCE is affiliated to Uttarakhand Technical University and recognised by the AICTE. The college is accredited with the NAAC A+ Grade. The college has ranked 68 under the BTech category by the Times Rankings 2025.

Shivalik College offers UG courses across various streams such as Engineering, Management, Science, and others. The flagship course offered at SCE is BTech. The college focuses on offering world-class education and generating resources for providing and supporting quality education. The college has an intensive and enabling academic environment with the mission to prepare students holistically. The top recruiters of Shivalik College of Engineering placements are Paytm, Infosys, TCS, Amazon, Google, Anchor, HCL, Autodesk, Tata Motors, and others.

Shivalik College of Engineering has collaborated with national organisations. The college appoints expert faculty members and aims to provide quality education to the students. Shivalik College admission is based on merit and entrance exams. The college accepts JEE Main and conducts the Shivaik Entrance Test. The college has top-notch infrastructure which is equipped with classrooms, labs, a library, medical, transport, a gym, a sports complex, a hostel, a mess, and others.
